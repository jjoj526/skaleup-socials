Featured Case Studies

Boosting Brand Awareness for a Local Cafe

A cozy cafe in downtown Austin wanted to increase its visibility and attract more customers through social media. We developed a content strategy focused on visually appealing food photography and engaging stories about the cafe's atmosphere and community events.

Results: 40% increase in followers, 25% rise in website traffic, and a noticeable uptick in foot traffic to the cafe.

Driving Sales for an E-commerce Store

An online clothing boutique aimed to boost its sales and reach a wider audience. We implemented targeted ad campaigns and influencer collaborations, showcasing the boutique's latest collections and promotions.

Results: 60% growth in sales, 50% expansion of customer base, and a significant improvement in return on ad spend.

Enhancing Engagement for a Tech Startup

A tech startup sought to build a strong online community and increase engagement with its target audience. We created a content calendar featuring informative posts, interactive polls, and behind-the-scenes glimpses into the company culture.

Results: 75% increase in engagement rate, 30% rise in social media mentions, and a more active and loyal online community.
